Strictly necessary cookies (e.g. your language preference) are set without consent. Analytics and advertising cookies are only set after you explicitly accept them via the cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser storage for this site.

We honour Google Consent Mode v2 — if you decline, no personal identifiers are sent to Google, only aggregate, anonymised pings.

Enquiry submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as the business relationship is active, or for up to 5 years after the last contact, whichever is sooner. Aggregate analytics data is retained per Google's default (2 months for events, 14 months for user-level reports).
