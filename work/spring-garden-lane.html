A boutique Wan Chai address where the metalwork carries from podium to penthouse. Feature wall, column cladding, suspended ceiling and balcony were all detailed by the same atelier so the building reads as one piece — no metallurgical mismatch between the lobby and the upper floors. Delivered for Wheelock Properties.

灣仔精品住宅項目，金屬構件貫穿底層平台至頂層。特色牆、柱身包板、懸掛天花、陽台由同一工坊細化處理，讓整棟讀來如一體——大堂與高層樓的金屬質感完全一致。為會德豐物業交付。
