Twenty-eight slender storeys above the King's Road tram line in North Point. Vertical bronze fins frame each balcony — a quiet rhythm against the older skyline of one of Hong Kong's most photographed tram-corridors. Designed by Ronald Lu & Partners for Wang On Properties.

英皇道電車軌道上的 28 層住宅，位於北角。每個陽台以垂直銅色竖梃包覆——在香港最常被拍攝的電車走廊之一的舊街景中織出安靜的金屬節奏。由 Ronald Lu & Partners 為宏安地產設計。
