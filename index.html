№ 08 — Process

Drawing. Mock-up.
Then production.

Eight steps, in two collaborative loops. No panel is mass-produced until the architect has signed off a 1:1 mock-up of every node.

Phase 01 Fabrication Drawing Collaboration 深化圖紙協作

01 Client

Shop Drawing & 3D Model

施工圖 / 3D 模型

The architect and curtain-wall consultant share the design intent — shop drawings, 3D models and tender documents.

02 Dongpai Metal

Fabrication Drawing

加工圖深化

We translate the design intent into manufacturing reality — every node, every joint, every tolerance.

03 Client

Review & Approval

圖紙審批

The drawing goes back to the consultant and architect. We mark-up, we re-issue, we sit in the meeting until every red mark is closed.

04 Dongpai Metal

Production

樣板生產

Approved drawings move to the production floor — laser, brake, weld, anodise and finish — to build the 1:1 mock-up.

Phase 02 Trial Mock-up Collaboration 樣板驗證協作

05 Client + Dongpai

Erect Mock-up

樣板組裝

The full-scale mock-up is erected — at our atelier, or on a designated site bay — for visual, weather and tolerance review.

06 Client + Dongpai

Installation Review

安裝復檢

We rehearse the install sequence on the mock-up — fixings, sealing, tolerances, water test. Adjustments fold back into the drawing.

07 Client

Drawing Sign-off

最終定稿

The mock-up becomes the contract. The architect signs the drawing-of-record — and only then is it released to production.

08 Dongpai Metal

Mass Production

批量生產

Production proper — with two QC checkpoints between each process. Same atelier, same supervisor, panel-to-handover.

№ 09 — Certification (ISO 9001 · EN 1090-2 EXC3 · CQM)

Engineered to EN 1090-2 · EXC3.

Our structural steel and curtain-wall fabrication is certified to the European norm EN 1090-2 Execution Class EXC3 — the class reserved for complex structures: high-rise façades, structures with seismic, fatigue or wind-fatigue loading, and elements where failure has serious safety consequences. It is the same execution class specified for landmark super-tall curtain-wall projects across Europe and Asia where façade failure would have public-safety consequences.

  • EXC3 Highest execution class for façade & structural members with fatigue, seismic or high-consequence loading.
  • CE CE-marking compliant. Documented welding-procedure specifications, NDT records and material traceability per panel.
  • QC Two QC checkpoints between each process: laser, brake, weld, anodise, finish. No piece leaves the atelier un-signed.
  • BD Hong Kong Buildings Department wind-load & Air Discharge Code conformant. Independent third-party testing on request.
